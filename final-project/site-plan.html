Site Name

Tourism in Menorca

Site Domain

Domain available: tourisminmenorca.com

Site Purpose

This website has the following objectives:

  • Establish relationships between all providers of tourist activities.

  • Collect events and activities offered by tourism activity providers.

  • Create a place where all those activities and events can be displayed.

  • Organize information and display it in a way that makes it easy to attend activities that people find interesting.

  • Allow citizens to report on upcoming events

Target Audience

This website will have mainly two types of audience.

  • The professional public in the field of tourism, that is, those interested in offering activities and coordinating with each other to ensure the largest number of attendees to the activities and events they plan.

  • Those looking for activities and events to attend

Professionals in the tourism sector

This audience expects a very professional website. The website must meet the expectations of business owners. This public is usually around 35-50 years old, men and women with a good economic position.

Activities and events seekers

This audience is broader, what they mainly seek is that the activities are well organized and that it is easy to access from any device with comfort to make a correct decision as to which activity to attend.
