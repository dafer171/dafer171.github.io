Purpose

The purpose of this website is, in the first place, to provide in real time relevant information about obviously the weather in your locality. But that's not all, we also seek to keep you informed of the main events that are taking place in the region and of course also those that will take place so that you can plan or not your attendance at them. It is to be usedas a planning and elaboration tool for the personal site to be developed this semester as part of the WDD 230 course.
