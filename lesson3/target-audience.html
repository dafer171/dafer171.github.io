Audience

This website is aimed at people who love new technologies and want to take advantage of them in their lives. Their age range will be between 15 and 50 years old. Economic situation: It does not matter because it will be a non-profit page. The main need for these people would be to know accurate information about the weather and events that take place in their community. This page should be used correctly on smartphones and tablets, also on desktops and laptops.
